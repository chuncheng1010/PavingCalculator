Safety
  1. Check all your equipment.
  2. Keep construction area clean & tidy at all times.
  3. Ensure that you read the operating manuals of all equipment including hired equipment such as plate compactors, brick-saws.
  4. Check the condition of the hand tools like hammers and chisels to make sure that they are in safe working order before use.
  5. Always operate equipment according to the manufactures instructions and conditions.
  6. All power tools should be operated through a safety switch board.
  7. Wear eye & ear protection at all times.
  8. Wear protective clothing, safety shoes, etc. at all times.
  9. Wear dust mask or breathing apparatus when cutting treated pine timber or masonry products to avoid breathing in dust particles.
  10. Locate local services within construction are before digging or excavating.
  11. Consult with the local authority about rules & regulations for construction guidelines in your local area for the job that you are performing.
  12. Always consult the manufactures for best practices guidelines for construction of the particular product that you are using.
