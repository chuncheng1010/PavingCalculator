How to Use Calculator

Preliminaries
  • Launch the application by pressing the launch icon on your device after downloading from store.
  • After launching the application a welcome screen will be displayed before the calculator begins to start.
Settings
  • User Preferences - Select the measurement system that you prefer either "Metric" or "Imperial" from the preferences. Press the SAVE icon located "top right" to save your settings.
  • Material & Labour Costs - Add or edit cost factors for quotations. Press the SAVE icon located "top right" to save changes.
  • Plant & Equipment - Add or Edit cost factors for quotations. Press the SAVE icon located "top right" to save changes.
Email Function
  • The email function works from either the "Answers" or "Quotations" page. By entering your project information into the text areas supplied you have the ability to send an email instantly.
Operating Calculator
  1. Paving Area - Enter the Width in the first box.
  2. Paving Area - Enter the Length in the second box.
  3. Paving Stone - Enter the Width in the third box.
  4. Paving Stone - Enter the Length in the fourth box.
  5. Road Base - Enter the Depth of the road base in the fifth box.
  6. Bedding Sand - Enter the Depth of the sand in the sixth box.
  7. Press "Calculator Answers" button to calculate.
  8. Press the SAVE icon "top right" to save your answers.
